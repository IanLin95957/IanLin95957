The Last Battle

After the downfall of the 3:rd reich, on the 5:th of May 1945 a strange battle took place in the
Tyrolean mountains in Itter, Austria.
Soldiers of the Wehrmacht and the US Army joined forces fought together against the SS to save
the lives of prisoners being held in the castle of Itter.
Under the command of American Captain John C Lee and German Major Josef Gangl the castle
was defended by less than 30 German and American soldiers together with the French former
prisoners of the castle.
Together, and with a little help from their Sherman Tank ”Besotten Jenny” who was placed at the
main entrance they managed to hold the 100-150 men strong Waffen SS unit at bay until they were
relieved by the allies.
